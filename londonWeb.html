London ON

Location

London is a city which locates in South Western Ontario in Canada, north of Lake Erie and the U.S. Border. It sits on the fork of the Thames River. It has 420.6 kmÂ² of land and is the 10th largest city in Canada.

Fun Facts

London is nicknamed "Forest City" because it was founded in the middle of a forest in 1836. It has over 200 parks and impressive forestations. London was once recommended by Simcoe to be the capital of Upper Canada, but was turned down and decided that Toronto should be the capital instead.

Population

London has a population of roughly 400 thousand, with 67% being in the working age, 16% of the population are under 15 years old, while and 17% of them over 65 years old.
